1: PROJECT

The project consists of the creation of a lamp with integration of LED lights, for them the laser cutter will be used, to obtain our pieces.

Issue

The extreme use of alternating current causing additional costs to the client as we also see in the lighting part.

Solution

Adapt to our lamp the set of led lights that help the client to be in an illuminated place or for special events coupled to a musical player.

2: SOCIAL IMPACT OF THE PROJECT

It is aimed at the general public, because the design is personalized, it is worked with LED diodes with the power to illuminate a certain area, thus avoiding the consumption of alternating current.
In the project was applied to recognize the components and know about each of them, as well as its operation.

3: MARKET STUDY

In the virtual market of Arequipa there are no products offered, and a simple design of similar lighting of 5 volts comes to have a high cost, this makes our product is new in the market.

The electrical circuit is located in the base of the product and this is made up of necessary components for the set of LED lights on the engraving, in addition an MP3 sound player will be attached.
